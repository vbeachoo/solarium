Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with a mass more than two and a half times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined. Jupiter is the third brightest natural object in the Earth's night sky. It was named after Jupiter, the chief deity of ancient Roman religion.
